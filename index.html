    Helping Hands Lab

    The Helping Hands Lab develops perception, planning, and control algorithms for robotic manipulation in unstructured domains. We are particularly interested in robots with the capability to assist humans in their native environments.

    Robots

    In the lab we primarily work with Universal Robot’s UR5 armed with a Robotiq 2F-85 Gripper. However, we have also worked with NASA’s Valkyrie, Clearpath’s Husky, and others.

    Boston, MA

    We are located on the 5th floor of the Interdisciplinary Science and Engineering Complex (ISEC) on Northeastern’s Boston campus.
